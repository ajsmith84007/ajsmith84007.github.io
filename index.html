My Profile

Background I am currently a Construction Manager for the NBN rollout across Victoria. My interest in IT started many years ago. From the time I picked up my first PC World magazine in 1997 and read it from cover to cover, I knew I was hooked on technology and everything it offered. Getting a Dial-up Internet connection to my house was of huge excitement although having it disconnect every time the phone rang was frustrating, as well as my parents restricting my Internet time to 1 hour per night. From here I learned how to build home computers and it wasn’t long before I had pulled apart and reassembled every PC I could get my hands on. Moving into high school and beyond, as technology progressed I become much more invested in IT. I could now build new PC’s and was learning about networking and basic programming. I became an Industrial Electrician and this opened up a new world of IT that I never new existed. I was now learning about machine Automation and programming in machine code such as C++ and Ladder Logic. I learned how to setup Industrial grade networking solutions, provided server installation and management through Windows Server 2003 and Linux. I choose to enrol in RMIT because in my current role with NBN I have seen many changes in our network infrastructure and I can see there will be many more improvements in networking and connectivity of everything to the internet in the coming years. I am completing the RMIT modules as a gateway into the IT degrees pathway and I’m hoping this will enable me to enrol in a full IT Degree next year. I am hoping to learn about Java programming as this is an area I have never looked at before. Also the general knowledge of how the IT industry works and what opportunities there are in the future for trained professionals.
