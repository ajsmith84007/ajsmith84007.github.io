Project Idea

Overview To utilize the Raspberry Pi portable computer as a way of accessing corporate networks for the purpose of network penetration testing. The handy ultraportable discreet device has the ability to hide in plain site inside a business or office with general staff possibly being unaware of its attachment to the corporate network. With Linux loaded onto the Raspberry Pi it should be possible to act as a gateway to the corporate network and provide remote access to the penetration tester.

Motivation The main goal of this project is to provide businesses and even home users with detailed reports outlining where their Network Security risks are. In the age of almost every business and home being connected to the internet, it is more important than ever to have your private data and internal networks secure from hackers and malicious software. The raspberry Pi would also be used to demonstrate the ease of social engineering as a hacking tool. Most office staff are all to willing to let complete strangers into their building, especially if that person is wearing hi-vis clothing or looks like a tradesperson. At this point the hacker could install the Raspberry Pi discreetly under a desk or even the Server Room, providing themselves with an internal gateway to the network. This solution will often bypass External Facing Firewalls and port blockers as devices connected internally are deemed safe for Server access.

Description Although my project is based on methods already deployed by other network penetration testing companies throughout the world, 1 key difference that I would like to deploy is training seminars and engagement with the IT teams of businesses and even home users as to how to better secure their networks and private information from people attempting to steal information. I believe with targeted and relevant training aimed at all internet users, could be a huge benefit in securing data. I propose each Raspberry Pi would be given to the customer at the end of the training sessions, with some goals set for the customer to test their own network with tools provided and ensure they are safe from further attacks. I believe this is a unique solution that is not yet readily available to businesses or home users and people are unaware of how at risk their data is. A raspberry Pi loaded with software such as Kali Linux which is a powerful penetration tool, one that can be utilized to remotely access any network it is attached to. Kali Linux Operating system builds that are readily available online and can come pre-loaded with numerous network penetration tools. From simple port scanners to blunt force password attacks on protected server content, the options available on Linux are limitless. Due to Linux being an open source platform, the ability to create your own tools and install them onto a distribution with no cost. This a huge benefit at keeping overheads low on a start-up network security businesses.

Tools & Technology The hardware requirements are simply a Raspberry Pi which is a very low-cost computer platform. The software loaded onto this device would be KALI Linux which already has numerous networking penetration tools available for testing. Website: Offensive-security.com. (2019). [online] Available at: https://www.offensive-security.com/kali-linux-arm-images/ [Accessed 13 Sep. 2019].

Skills Required Skills required for this project would be an in depth understanding of KALI Linux software. General knowledge of Raspberry Pi hardware and the ability to put together training packages that can be aimed at different levels of learners. i.e. IT professionals and Server admins vs general home users and or tech savvy individuals.

Outcome This project has the potential to create a new business opportunity with the ideal outcome being successfully deployed network security consultant, who would promote the business to potential clients throughout the country. The impact of this business in an internet of things connected world could be huge, people need more education and training into how to protect themselves online. We all hear of that friend or colleague that has had their identity stolen or credit card information stolen. To even in worse case scenarios where businesses are held ransom from millions of dollars as there data is locked away behind a crypto pay wall. The main purpose and ideal outcome for this project would be a better education of how at-risk networks and computer systems can be. A Networking security business that is able to provide low-cost tailored solutions to individuals and IT Support staff, with customised training packages that offer different content depending on the target demographic.
